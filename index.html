Introduction

This page documents my personal experience involving the sale of a Play Store Console account. It is published solely to raise awareness and help others make informed decisions.

All information shared below is factual and supported by available evidence.

Summary of the Incident

  • Item sold: Play Store Console Account.
  • Agreement: Full payment after account transfer.
  • Outcome: Account transferred, payment not received.
  • Previous Status: Buyer stopped responding after receiving access.
  • Current Status: After knocking again n again, they returned a suspended account and saying they can not do anything now.

Timeline of Events

  • Dec 15–28, 2025: Initial contact and agreement reached
  • Dec 28, 2025: Console access provided
  • Jan 04, 2026: Payment deadline missed
  • Following days: Multiple follow-ups with no response

Current Status

As of publication, no payment has been received despite repeated attempts to resolve the matter amicably, including offering a compromise amount. The console was later returned in a terminated and suspended state, rendering it unusable.

I offered a reasonable compromise when the buyer stated they were unable to pay the agreed amount of INR 1.5 lakh. However, they were only willing to pay INR 5,000 while retaining full access to the console.

Even after the console was terminated by Google, I attempted further compromise. Following this, all communication from the buyer ceased.
